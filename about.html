About

My story

I am Kate Koyama, a brazilian creative multimedia designer located in France. My path into design started in Brazil where I started my studies in industrial design which was later transferred to the United States. In the USA, I continue my studies but with a focus on graphic design. Following my graduation, I worked in a multi-disciplinary agency for a couple of years until deciding to pursue a master education in Switzerland, land of graphic design.

After receiving a dual-master degree, I worked for a software company where my interest in user interface design and user experience grew. This interest led me to further my education by learning more about user experience design. Today I can my apply the skills I have gathered throughout the years to my current job as a UX designer.

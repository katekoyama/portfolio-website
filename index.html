Projects

Pronto meal planning app

Being a working parent has many joys but as well as many challenges. Work does not stop when you get back home, especially with young kids.

This app allows working parents to better plan their meals so that they can enjoy family time better. With Pronto, they can find healthy and nutritious recipes according to their family preferences as well as how long the meal preparation takes.

Culturescape poster series

Culturescape is a yearly event that takes place in Switzerland, which aims to promote intercultural communication, collaboration and sharing. For this project three posters were created to represent the cultural environments of each of the following countries: China, Iran and the United States.

View project

Energiewende animations

Energiewende (Energy transition in english) is an exhibition made for the Deutsches Museum in Munich. The goal of the project was to create four animations to inform visitors about issues such as emissions trading, nuclear energy, storage of nuclear waste and the concept of smart homes.

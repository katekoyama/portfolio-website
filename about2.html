About me

I am Kate Koyama, a brazilian creative multimedia designer located in France. My path into design started in Brazil where I started my studies in industrial design which was later transferred to the United States. In the USA, I continued my studies but with a focus on graphic design. Following my graduation, I worked in a multi-disciplinary agency for a couple of years until deciding to pursue a master education in Switzerland, land of graphic design.

Today I work as a UX designer at a sofware company in Basel, Switzerland.

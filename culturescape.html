Culturescape

Culturescape is a yearly event that takes place in Switzerland, which aims to promote intercultural communication, collaboration and sharing.

For this project three posters were created to represent the cultural environments of each of the following countries: China, Iran and the United States.

My process

Project brief

Culturescape is a yearly event that takes place in Switzerland, which aims to promote intercultural communication, collaboration and sharing of different countries. The chosen countries for this project were China, Iran and the United States.

Goal of the project

The goals of this project was to create a poster series that could reflect this cultural immersion that takes place in different areas of the country.

Process

The visual exploration of the theme aimed to use the swiss flag as a point of entry, so the emblematic white cross in the swiss flag served as a window into the culture of the represented country. Although representing the culture of a country is an enormous task, I aimed to incorporate celebrities in literature, television, cinema, music and other areas. This was represented through the photo collage placed inside the cross.

To also visually depict the country in question, elements of the flag were used in a more free manner. This added to the visual impact of the posters.

Conclusion

The series was then printed using silkscreen which rendered the colors more vibrant and impactful. The final results were shared with other students and received a good review.

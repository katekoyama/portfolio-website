Pronto app

Pronto—Your meal app that keeps you on track—allows working parents to better plan their meals so that they can enjoy family time better. With Pronto, they can find healthy and nutritious recipes according to their family preferences as well as how long the meal preparation takes.

For this project, I was asked to research the theme of house hold management and which solution could help people lives in regards to their home. After conducting interview, it quickly became clear to me that meal planning was a big part of people's household management. The Pronto app was conceived to help working parents manage this aspect of their daily lives better.

My process

Research

In order to better understand the planning of meals of working parents, a user research was conducted. The discussion guide used had questions in five main groups: household management, meal preparation planning, schedule, coordination and budget. This allowed us to create a persona.

Ideation

In this phase a user flow was created to help visualize possible paths the user might take. This process allowed me to create the necessary screens for the next step.

Conclusion

After having worked on the ideation the next step was to translate the solution into sketches at first which were then tested with the targeted group. After the usability testing, these paper sketches were reiterated upon and then transformed into a low fidelity prototype which was again tested with the users.
